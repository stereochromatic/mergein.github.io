Transform how your business connects with potential customers

With just two clicks, people can place real-time video/audio calls and send SMS messages to an organizations brick and mortar locations & departments near them or anywhere in the world.

Connect

Create a company profile and send invites to your employees & add them to a location and departments. So they can accept & make real-time video calls and SMS messages.

Respond

Share your company's MergeIn account with your social media channels or attach a link on your e-commerce website.

Convert

Start accepting incoming & outgoing calls- SMS messages from potential customers and allow them to add whatever department or location their connected with to their favorites list.

Features tailored for business

  • Allow sales reps to send invites to customers. So both parties can have an instant connection with each other.
  • Accept and make incoming calls & SMS messages from people within your organizations and customers
  • Place callers from over the web on hold and transfer them to any department or person within your company
  • Add different location, branches and departments to your official account. So people over the web can connect with any section of your business instantly

Benefits

  • Be the first store shoppers enter before they take a trip to the mall and show what’s on display at that particular location.
  • Save your customers time by already having their items on hold for in-store picks ups
  • No download required for customers to merge-in and no extra labor on employees
  • Stay connected with your customers for a lifetime
